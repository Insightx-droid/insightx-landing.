ML for Business Track

This track empowers professionals to apply machine learning to solve real business challenges. Each module focuses on data-driven strategies using practical ML techniques.

Module 1: Predictive Analytics for Business Growth

Learn to build regression models in Python to forecast sales, revenue, and customer lifetime value. Hands-on with datasets, visualization, and model tuning.

Module 2: Customer Segmentation with Clustering

Use K-Means and Hierarchical Clustering to segment customer data and build targeted strategies. Analyze real CRM datasets and interpret clusters.

Module 3: Churn Prediction Modeling

Create classification models using decision trees, logistic regression, and ensemble methods to predict customer churn and take preventive actions.

Module 4: Recommendation Engines

Design collaborative filtering and content-based recommendation systems to enhance product discovery and personalization in e-commerce or services.

Module 5: Time Series Forecasting for Operations

Apply ARIMA, Prophet, and LSTM models to predict demand, inventory levels, and staffing needs in real business cases.
