AI in Marketing Track

This track is designed for marketers, students, and professionals who want to gain practical skills in leveraging AI for powerful marketing strategies. It includes hands-on projects, tools, and case-based learning.

Module 1: AI-Powered Social Media Campaigns

Learn how to create and manage social media campaigns using generative AI tools like ChatGPT, Copy.ai, and Canva AI. You'll build a campaign strategy, generate creatives, and automate posting schedules.

Module 2: Personalized Email Marketing with AI

Implement tools like Mailchimp + AI integrations to send customized emails based on user behavior, preferences, and predictive analytics.

Module 3: Customer Journey Mapping with AI

Use AI analytics and visualization tools to understand and map out complete customer journeys and design effective touchpoints.

Module 4: AI-Powered Content Creation

Generate blog posts, landing pages, and ad copies using tools like Jasper, Notion AI, and GrammarlyGO. Learn prompt engineering and tone customization.

Module 5: Marketing Automation and AI Funnels

Design automated sales funnels with no-code AI tools (e.g., Pabbly, Make, Zapier) and lead nurturing workflows driven by smart triggers and data analysis.
