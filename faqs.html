Frequently Asked Questions

What is PRACTIQ?

PRACTIQ Learning Lab is an online virtual platform offering hands-on, project-based learning experiences in Marketing, AI, ML, and related domains.

Who can join PRACTIQ Lab?

Students, working professionals, founders, and educators looking to upskill in AI, Marketing, and Machine Learning are welcome to join.

Are the courses beginner-friendly?

Yes. Courses are designed to accommodate both beginners and experienced learners. You'll find tracks tailored to your skill level.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You'll build real-world projects such as AI marketing campaigns, ML business dashboards, and growth automation workflows.

Do I need a technical background to enroll?

No. There is no requirement for technical background for non-tech learners. Labs are designed to cater both tech and non-tech learners for advanced learning.

Is there a certification provided?

Yes. Upon successful completion of each lab or course, participants receive a digital certificate of completion from PRACTIQ.

How long are the courses?

Courses typically 12 weeks long depending on the lab, with flexible schedules and self-paced options available.

How do I apply or enroll?

Visit the Join Now page to register and receive access to lab content and schedules.

Is there any cost involved?

Yes. Labs involve a fee. This information will be provided to the lab member by academic counsellor.

Can I access the labs on mobile?

Yes, the platform is responsive and accessible from desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
