Frequently Asked Questions

What is InsightX Lab?

InsightX Lab is an online virtual platform offering hands-on, project-based learning experiences in Marketing, AI, ML, and related domains.

Who can join InsightX Lab?

Students, working professionals, founders, and educators looking to upskill in AI, Marketing, and Machine Learning are welcome to join.

Are the courses beginner-friendly?

Yes. Courses are designed to accommodate both beginners and experienced learners. You'll find tracks tailored to your skill level.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You'll build real-world projects such as AI marketing campaigns, ML business dashboards, no-code tools, and growth automation workflows.

Do I need a technical background to enroll?

No. We offer no-code AI and business-focused labs for non-tech professionals. Technical labs are also available for advanced learners.

Is there a certification provided?

Yes. Upon successful completion of each lab or course, participants receive a digital certificate of completion from InsightX Lab.

How long are the courses?

Courses typically range from 2 to 8 weeks depending on the lab, with flexible schedules and self-paced options available.

How do I apply or enroll?

Visit the Join Now page to register and receive access to lab content and schedules.

Is there any cost involved?

Many labs and events are free. Premium labs may involve a small fee which will be mentioned on the respective course pages.

Can I access the labs on mobile?

Yes, the platform is responsive and accessible from desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
